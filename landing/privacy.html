Privacy Policy

AntiCasino Shield is designed as a local Windows self-control tool.

  • The app works locally on your computer.
  • It does not collect personal data.
  • It does not send browser history to a server.
  • It does not track users or use analytics.
  • Logs are stored locally in the app data folder.
  • Donation and payment links are handled by third-party services such as Buy Me a Coffee, Ko-fi or PayPal.

For privacy questions, contact Ivan Derkach through the official GitHub project page or Telegram channel.
